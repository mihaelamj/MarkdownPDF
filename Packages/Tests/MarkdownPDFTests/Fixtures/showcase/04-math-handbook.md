# A Handbook of Display Mathematics

A dense witness for the opt-in TeX-math subset: scripts, fractions, radicals,
sums, integrals, accents, and operators, with multilingual captions.

## Scripts and indices

$$x^2 \qquad x_i^2 \qquad a_{i,j}^{n+1} \qquad e^{x^2} \qquad 2^{2^{x}}$$

## Fractions and radicals

$$\frac{a}{b} \qquad \frac{1}{1 + \frac{1}{1 + x}} \qquad \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$$

$$\varphi = \frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2}$$

## Sums, products, and limits

$$\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$$

$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1$$

## Relations and Greek symbols

Inline: $a \leq b$, $x \in S$, $p \approx q$, $\alpha + \beta = \gamma$,
$\theta \to \pi$, $\Sigma$ and $\Omega$. The roots of $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$:

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$
